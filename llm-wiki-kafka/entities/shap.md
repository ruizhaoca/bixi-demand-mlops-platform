# Entity: SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations)

A model-agnostic explainability method based on game-theoretic Shapley values — a tool in [[responsible-ai]].

- Attributes a prediction to its features via Shapley values; both local (per-prediction) and global understanding.
- Listed among model-agnostic post-hoc methods in [[model-explainability]] alongside [[lime|LIME]], PDP, ICE, permutation importance.
- Appears as a **fairness** auditing tool in [[fairness-bias-ml]] and an implied **security** defense (explainability) in [[securing-ml-applications]].
