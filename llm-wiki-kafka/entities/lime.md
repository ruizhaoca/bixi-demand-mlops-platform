# Entity: LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations)

A model-agnostic explainability method — a tool in [[responsible-ai]].

- Explains an individual prediction by fitting a simple **local surrogate** model around it (a "local explanation" technique).
- Works with any black-box classifier/regressor, incl. text and image; integrated in ELI5's TextExplainer.
- Listed in [[model-explainability]] and used for **fairness** auditing in [[fairness-bias-ml]]. Pairs with [[shap|SHAP]].

Appears in: [[model-explainability]], [[fairness-bias-ml]]
